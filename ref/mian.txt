1. Executive Vision

Digifort Labs is an enterprise-grade Medical Records Department (MRD) ecosystem designed for the Indian healthcare sector. We provide a seamless hybrid solution that manages massive physical paper archives alongside a secure, digital-first PDF repository. The platform is built to ensure hospitals exceed the requirements of the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023 and NABH/NABL accreditation.

3. Core Product Modules

3.1 Digital MRD (Cloud Archiving)

Patient-Wise Repository: Digital folders indexed by Hospital UHID and linked to ABHA IDs.

Smart Compression: Integrated Gzip engine that compresses PDFs before AWS S3 upload.

Secure Viewer: Browser-based, watermarked PDF viewer with dynamic watermarking.

3.2 Advanced Physical Warehouse Management

Interactive Heat Map: Visual occupancy monitoring with Green/Orange/Red status indicators.

Rack-Level Analytics: "Deep-dive" into specific racks to view file lists and available slots.

Physical File Search & Highlight: Real-time highlighting of exact Aisle and Rack locations for quick retrieval.

3.3 QR & Labeling Engine

Thermal Label Generation: High-density QR codes for physical folders.

Handheld Scanner Integration: "Check-In" and "Check-Out" workflows for physical chain-of-custody.
